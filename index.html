“You are what you listen to”

13 . 12 . 2022

What is your favourite song?

My guest explains why their favourite banger means so much to them and how it kickstarted their career.
Also in this episode, we talk about mental health, aspirations, and blue sky thinking.
We deep dive into the importance of listening. Whether listening to colleagues, customers, or our audience, we hear you and your thoughts.

Key takeaways:

  • The power of music
  • Mental health is just as important as phyisical health
  • Nurture your connections
  • Have fun
